How to Play

Help Reeborg navigate through the hurdles to reach the green goal!

  1. Drag code blocks from the left panel into the "Your Code" area.
  2. Arrange blocks in the order you want them to be executed.
  3. Click "Run Program" to see Reeborg follow your instructions.
  4. If you make a mistake, click "Reset" to try again.

Challenge: use move, jump, turn, repeat and condition blocks to pass hurdles and reach the goal with fewer blocks.
